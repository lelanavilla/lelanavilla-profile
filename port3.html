Fine Artist Turned Developer

A passion for creation has always been my driving force. I caught the coding bug while attempting to figure out a way for my 90 year old grandfather to use and understand technology, so he would be able to stay connected to the rest of the family. During this process, I feel in love with CSS, and quickly came to the understanding that coding was art that does something. So I decided to take my gift of being able to learn rapidly, and have taken off on a journey to learn all I can about coding, and see where it takes me, and I couldnt be more excited.
