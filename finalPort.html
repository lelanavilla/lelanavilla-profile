Ongoing Project

Ask GOO GOO (personal project currently in development)

Background: A year and a half ago, I sat back watching my 92 year old grandfather sit and stare at the tablet his daugther had purchased for him. I knew my grandfather wanted to connect to his family. He loved getting pictures, and talking on the phone, and he knew that his tablet offered him those abilities, but despite his efforts, he just could not understand some of the concepts I had gone over with him,combine that with some of the limitiations that come with being 92, his frustration was real. So I created an application that had senior friendly UI elements, single button that displayed all social media account inboxs together, and grabbed family members social media profile photos, and created large icons with the photo in a phonebook like app. My grandfather was completely thrilled. Looking back now I can say this is when my hobby became my passion. Being able to see how my simple app was allowing him to stay connected to the family, and remembering his ear to ear grin, he'd wear each time he would use his tablet, because it "made him feel young", he would tell everyone, Ill never forget that. In March 2018, my grandfather passed away, while I was away finishing my schooling to become a Full Stack Developer. The application that I had created for him, was on his tablet, and we joke he must've taken it with him to heaven, because to date, its still MIA. So, in his memory, I have decided to recreate this application but with more features based on suggestions from my grandfather, and document the process as I go, the following is that process:

Concept: An application that simplifies everyday tasks for seniors.

Features this app will include: UI elements that are optimized for this demographic( bigger buttons, larger text, less options, voice to text searching, large icons of family members photos displaying in the phonebook and next to messages.

Possible Future Features: A private network with site specific email addresses, and online calling feature like facebook offers for small family groups. Possibly with the creation of a single, shared group wall page, each member of the family has access to, for posting messages and pics for all family members to see. A Thing finder app, a Whats Their Name app, and an Ask Goo Goo app (make sure to ask me the story behind the application's name).
